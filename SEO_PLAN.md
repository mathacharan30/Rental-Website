## Phase 4 — Blog Content

> **Start week 2–3. Publish 1 post per week.**

Blog content builds long-term organic traffic through informational keywords. Compounds over 3–6 months.

### Recommended First 6 Posts (in order)

1. **Bridal Lehenga Rental vs Buying in 2026 — Real Cost Comparison**
   - Target keyword: `bridal lehenga rental vs buying`
   - Angle: Show the math — ₹500–₹3,000 rental vs ₹40,000+ purchase + storage + cleaning

2. **Complete Bridal Outfit Checklist for a South Indian Wedding**
   - Target keyword: `South Indian bridal outfit checklist`
   - Angle: Practical guide covering clothing, jewellery, makeup, photography. Link to all 4 service pages.

3. **10 Best Wedding Venues in Mysuru (2026 Updated)**
   - Target keyword: `wedding venues in Mysuru`
   - Angle: Useful roundup for every Mysuru bride. Builds topical authority in the city.

4. **Bridal Lehenga Colours for Different Skin Tones — A Styling Guide**
   - Target keyword: `bridal lehenga colour guide`
   - Angle: Fashion/styling advice. Positions People & Style as an expert, not just a rental shop.

5. **How Much Does a Full Bridal Look Cost in Bangalore? (2026 Breakdown)**
   - Target keyword: `bridal package cost Bangalore`
   - Angle: Price transparency article. High purchase intent — people searching this are ready to book.

6. **Dasara Wedding Season in Mysuru — Booking Guide for Brides**
   - Target keyword: `Dasara wedding Mysuru`
   - Angle: Seasonal, local, unique. Strong local authority signal. No competitor is writing this.
